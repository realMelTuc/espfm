Features
Everything you need to run EVE infrastructure

From fuel management to profitability analysis — ESPFM covers the full operational lifecycle of Upwell structures.

⛽
Fuel Planning
Accurate burn-rate calculations using EVE's official consumption tables. Days-remaining gauges with visual fill levels and snapshot history.
📊
Profitability Analysis
Monthly P&L per structure. Compare fuel cost against income streams. Status badges: profitable, break-even, or loss.
💰
Income Tracking
Log industry tax, market tax, reprocessing tax, moon rental, and office income. 30-day rolling summaries per structure.
⚡
Service Modules
Configure online services per structure. Each adds 10 fuel blocks/hr. Toggle online/offline and see the live fuel impact.
📅
Replenishment Calendar
Visual calendar showing fuel run-out dates and 7-day advance alerts. Never miss a fuel delivery again.
🏗
Structure Registry
Full structure database: type, system, security class, owner corp, state, and fuel bay capacity. Supports all Upwell structure types.
