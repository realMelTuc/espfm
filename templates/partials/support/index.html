Getting Started
  1. Go to Dashboard → click Initialize DB Tables to create the database schema.
  2. Go to Settings and set a fuel block price (check Jita sell price for current value).
  3. Go to Structures → click + Add Structure to register your first structure.
  4. Go to Services → add your online service modules for that structure.
  5. Go to Fuel Planning → click Update Fuel to enter current stock.
  6. Go to Income → log your tax income from wallet journal exports.
  7. Go to Profitability → click Record Snapshots to save a P&L baseline.
